Accommodation Guidelines

- Residents must follow the code of conduct.
- Quiet hours: 10 PM - 7 AM.
- Visitors allowed only from 9 AM - 9 PM.
- Smoking, alcohol, and illegal substances are prohibited.

- Apply online via the UNITEN Accommodation portal.
- Required documents: Student ID, proof of enrollment.
- Applications open one month before the semester starts.
- Room allocation is based on availability and preferences.

- Fees vary based on room type.
- Payments can be made via online banking or cash at the finance office.
- Refunds are available if cancellations are made within 14 days.
- Late payment may result in a penalty.

- Each room includes a bed, desk, chair, and wardrobe.
- WiFi is available throughout the accommodation.
- Maintenance requests can be submitted online.
- Students are responsible for keeping their rooms clean.
