This website helps UNITEN students find, apply for, and manage their on-campus accommodation easily.

You can apply by logging into your student account, selecting your preferred accommodation type, and your preferred room.

Applications usually open before each semester. We recommend applying as early as possible to secure your preferred room.

The accommodation includes WiFi, air conditioning, shared kitchens, laundry services, and security.
